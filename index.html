You're in the U.S. on a student visa and are about to graduate: now what?

You've overcome the hurdles of getting into a university in the United States—and of simply getting into the United States for that matter—itself no small feat. You're in your final semester after several months or years of adjusting to life in a new country. You have been entertaining the idea of remaining in the country after graduation. The legal immigration options available to you as an F-1 visa-holder, however, are very limited. But they do exist!

The Options

Below is the list of the most common options available to F-1 students who would like to remain in the United States after graduation. Click on the options for further details about each one.
