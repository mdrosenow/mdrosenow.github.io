Putting that degree to use

Students who have completed a degree program (undergraduate, graduate, post-graduate) are eligibile to work for up to 12 months in full-time practical training. The area of work must be "directly related" to the student's major. Meet with your Designated School Official (DSO) several months before graduation so that he or she may help you file for a work permit in advance of your program end date.

Special Rules for STEM degress

Those students earning qualifying Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics degrees (STEM degrees) are eligible for an additional 17 months of OPT on top of the 12 months initially granted.

Some of the most common stem degrees are the following:

  • Computer Programming
  • Accounting
  • Civil Engineering
  • Psychology
  • Statistics
