Marriage to a United States citizen

If you genuinely fall in love with a United States citizen at any time during your stay in the United States and you get married to that United States citizen, you will be eligible for lawful permanent residence (otherwise known as a green card).

Special rule for spouses of Cuban nationals

This also applies to marriage to a Cuban national who is residing in the United States. Thanks to special privileges available through the Cuban Adjustment Act, spouses of Cuban nationals are eligible for a green card.

Don't get married to someone only to secure legal status

Don't fall into the trap of marrying someone just for the papers. That's a federal crime and you may end up walking down the wrong aisle, that of a federal prison. Punishment can be as steep as a five-year prison sentence and up to $250,000 in fines.
